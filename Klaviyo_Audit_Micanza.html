Action Plan

Recommendations & Next Steps

  • →Improve campaign open rate (41.4% → Poor): Test subject lines and sending times. The Spring Sale Follow-Up (34.7% OR) already shows list fatigue from high send frequency.
  • →Increase flow share of revenue (currently 33.9%): Abandoned Cart is performing excellently, but Browse Abandonment and Post-Purchase flows have room to grow. Activate missing flows (Added to Cart, First-to-Second Purchase).
  • →Fix high unsubscribe rates in flows: ETQ Boxer Shorts (1.8%), Customer Thank You (1.6%), Shopify Footer #2 (1.6%) — review content and timing of these flows.
  • →Deactivate or rebuild the Sunset Flow: 15.9% open rate and €0 revenue from 69 delivered emails — this flow is effectively dead and is harming sender reputation.
  • →Segment product-focus campaigns: The Shades campaigns achieve very low click rates (0.30–0.48%) despite 100k+ recipients — targeted segmentation by product interest would improve both relevance and revenue.
  • →Monitor Klaviyo share: At 35.24%, the account is above the 30% benchmark — not a critical issue, but worth keeping an eye on.
